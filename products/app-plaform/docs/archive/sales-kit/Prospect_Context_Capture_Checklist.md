# Prospect Context Capture Checklist

Version: 1.0  
Status: Minimal checklist for collecting safe prospect-specific facts before customizing inserts  
Last revised: March 1, 2026

---

## Purpose

Use this checklist before creating a named prospect insert.

It keeps the sales material specific without crossing into invented or unsupported claims.

---

## Required Inputs

1. Prospect name
2. Buyer type
3. Known workflow focus
4. Any verified public facts you are willing to cite

---

## Buyer Type Options

- broker / DP
- merchant banker
- listed issuer
- mixed / unclear

If the buyer type is mixed or unclear, start from the general insert instead of forcing a specific profile.

---

## Useful Verified Facts To Collect

- license category or operating role
- whether the prospect handles brokerage, DP, issue management, issuer filing, or corporate actions
- whether the prospect appears active in IPO / FPO / rights activity
- any stated digital or reporting pain in public materials
- any known pressure point from the user’s relationship notes

Only include items you can verify or that the user explicitly confirms.

---

## Safe Customization Boundary

You can safely tailor:

- emphasis,
- likely pain ordering,
- pilot scope,
- the recommended insert to send.

You should not invent:

- exact transaction volumes,
- exact filing failures,
- exact internal tooling issues,
- exact regulator interactions,
- exact margin-service status,
- named upcoming issues or corporate actions unless verified.

---

## Output Rule

Once the checklist is complete:

1. choose the closest base insert,
2. use [Named_Prospect_Insert_Template.md](./Named_Prospect_Insert_Template.md),
3. keep all unsupported details as assumptions or leave them blank.
