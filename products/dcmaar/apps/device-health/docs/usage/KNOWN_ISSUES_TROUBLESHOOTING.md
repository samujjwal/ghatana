# DCMaar – Device Health Extension – Known Issues & Troubleshooting

## 1. Known Issues

- Browser-specific quirks may affect metrics availability.
- Connectivity issues can impact metric freshness.

## 2. Troubleshooting

- When metrics do not appear, check:
  - Extension permissions and status.
  - Backend/agent connectivity.
  - Plugin configuration and logs.

This document is self-contained and lists common issues and mitigations for the Device Health extension.
