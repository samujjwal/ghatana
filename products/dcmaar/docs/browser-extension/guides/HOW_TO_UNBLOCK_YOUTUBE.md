# How to Unblock YouTube in Guardian Extension

## Quick Steps to Unblock YouTube

### Method 1: Using the Settings Page (Easiest)

1. **Open Guardian Settings**
   - Click the Guardian extension icon in your browser toolbar
   - Click the settings/gear icon in the popup, OR
   - Right-click on the Guardian icon → Options

2. **Navigate to "Blocking Policies" Tab**
   - You'll see multiple tabs: General, Performance Budgets, Alerts, **Blocking Policies**, Data & Export
   - Click on the **"Blocking Policies"** tab

3. **Expand Your Policy**
   - You'll see a list of blocking policies
   - Click on the policy that's blocking YouTube (usually something like "Parental Controls" or "Work Hours Block")
   - It will expand to show details

4. **Find YouTube and Unblock It**
   - Look for **"Blocked Domains"** section
   - You should see `youtube.com` listed there
   - Click the green **"Unblock"** button next to `youtube.com`

### Method 2: Quick Unblock Feature (Fastest)

1. Go to the Blocking Policies tab (steps 1-2 above)
2. Expand the policy blocking YouTube
3. Scroll to **"Quick Unblock"** section at the bottom
4. Type: `youtube.com`
5. Click **"Unblock"** button

**YouTube is now unblocked! 🎉**

---

## Alternative: Enable/Disable Entire Policy

If you want to temporarily allow all blocked sites (including YouTube):

1. Go to Blocking Policies tab
2. Look for the policy blocking content
3. **Toggle the checkbox** on the left to **disable the policy**
4. The policy is now inactive (all sites allowed)

To re-enable blocking later, just toggle it back on.

---

## Understanding Blocking Policies

### Policy Structure:
- **Blocked Categories**: Groups like YouTube (Streaming), Facebook (Social), etc.
- **Blocked Domains**: Specific websites blocked by this policy
- **Allowed Exceptions**: Domains that are whitelisted (allowed even if their category is blocked)
- **Time Windows**: When the blocking is active (e.g., 9 AM - 5 PM)

### Example:
If you have a policy that blocks "Streaming" category (which includes YouTube), you can add YouTube to "Allowed Exceptions" to unblock it while keeping other streaming sites blocked.

---

## UI Components in Blocking Policies Tab

### For Each Policy, You Can:

- ✅ **Enable/Disable** - Toggle checkbox to activate/deactivate blocking
- 🔓 **Unblock Domains** - Click "Unblock" button next to any blocked site
- 🔒 **Remove from Allowlist** - Delete domains you previously added as exceptions
- ⚡ **Quick Unblock** - Fast input field to unblock any domain
- 📋 **View Blocked Categories** - See which content categories are blocked
- ⏰ **View Time Windows** - See when blocking is active

## If YouTube Still Appears Blocked

### Troubleshooting:

1. **Reload the page** after unblocking
2. **Clear browser cache** (Ctrl+Shift+Delete or Cmd+Shift+Delete)
3. **Hard refresh** the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
4. **Check multiple policies** - You might have multiple blocking policies; unblock YouTube in all of them
5. **Check time windows** - If the policy has time windows, YouTube might be blocked only during specific hours

## Support

If you're still having issues unblocking YouTube:

1. **Check Console Logs**: Open DevTools (F12), go to Console tab, look for error messages
2. **Verify Policy is Active**: Make sure the policy that's blocking YouTube is actually enabled
3. **Check Blocked Categories**: Confirm `youtube.com` is listed under "Blocked Domains" or "Streaming" category
4. **Reload Extension**: Unload and reload the extension from `chrome://extensions/`
