## Purpose

Use this template when you have a real target institution and verified context.

It is designed to prevent invented prospect facts.

Only fill in prospect-specific details that are:

- provided directly by the user,
- confirmed from the prospect’s own public materials,
- or otherwise documented in a primary source you are willing to cite.

---

## Safe-Use Rules

1. Do not state that the prospect has a specific license class, workflow, or pain point unless it is known.
2. Do not change the legal or factual anchors from the claims pack unless you add a new verified source first.
3. Keep all prospect-specific assumptions explicitly labeled as assumptions.
4. If you do not know something, leave the placeholder in place or convert it to a neutral description.
