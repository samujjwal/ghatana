# Virtual-Org – Workflow Library – Known Issues & Troubleshooting

## 1. Known Issues

- **Complex workflows are harder to reason about** – Nested or highly dynamic workflows can be difficult to understand and test.

## 2. Troubleshooting Scenarios

### 2.1 Unexpected Workflow State or Behavior

- **Checks**:
  - Review workflow definitions and mappings to operators and Virtual‑Org components.
  - Inspect logs for errors or unexpected transitions.
