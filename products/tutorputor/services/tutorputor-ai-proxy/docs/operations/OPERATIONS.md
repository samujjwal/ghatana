# Tutorputor AI Proxy Service – Operations Guide

## 1. Overview

The Tutorputor AI Proxy service runs as part of the Tutorputor backend and fronts external AI providers.

## 2. Deployment & Configuration

- Deploy alongside other Tutorputor services.
- Configure:
  - Provider endpoints and credentials.
  - Rate limits, timeouts, and safety policies.

## 3. Monitoring

- Track call volume, latency, error rates, and provider-specific failures.

This guide is self-contained and documents operational considerations for the Tutorputor AI Proxy service.
