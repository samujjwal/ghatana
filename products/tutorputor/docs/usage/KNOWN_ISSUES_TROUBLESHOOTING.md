# Tutorputor – Known Issues & Troubleshooting

## 1. Known Issues

- Complex multi-step flows may be sensitive to changes in contracts or services.

## 2. Troubleshooting

- When flows break:
  - Check contract definitions and compatibility.
  - Inspect service logs for orchestration and state errors.

This document is self-contained and lists common issues and mitigations for Tutorputor.
