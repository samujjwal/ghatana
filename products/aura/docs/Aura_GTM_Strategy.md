# Aura Go-To-Market Strategy

## Market Entry: Beauty-First

Aura enters the market through the **beauty consumer segment** — specifically skincare and foundation shade matching — where ingredient complexity, shade fit anxiety, and influencer fatigue create a clear pull for intelligent, trustworthy recommendations.

**Initial target user:** Women 18–34 who actively research beauty products online before purchasing, and who have experienced at least one of: wrong shade purchase, ingredient reaction, or influencer recommendation disappointment.

---

## Distribution & Acquisition Channels

### Primary (Creator-Led)

| Channel                      | Approach                                                                                                                            | Rationale                                                                          |
| ---------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| TikTok beauty creators       | Partner with skincare and shade-matching focused creators who demonstrate Aura's ingredient and shade analysis in authentic content | High reach with primary demographic; video format shows the "aha" moment naturally |
| YouTube beauty reviewers     | In-depth ingredient and shade comparison videos referencing Aura analysis                                                           | Decision-making content; users in research mode                                    |
| Instagram skincare community | Influencer posts demonstrating Aura's "ingredient safety check" feature                                                             | Visual product comparison; strong engagement for skincare content                  |

### Secondary (Community-Led)

| Channel                                                              | Approach                                                                                                        |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Reddit skincare communities (r/SkincareAddiction, r/MakeupAddiction) | Organic participation through authentic helpful answers; Aura tool links where genuinely useful                 |
| Beauty-focused Discord servers                                       | Community presence; not promotional — provide value first                                                       |
| Press & editorial                                                    | Beauty editor outreach with product-specific data stories (e.g., "most common hidden allergens in foundations") |

### Performance Marketing (Phase 2)

Paid social advertising activates once organic retention benchmarks are met (30-day retention ≥ 25%) to avoid paying to acquire users into a leaky bucket.

## Growth Loops

### Loop 1 — Content Discovery

Users save products → recommendations improve → users share saves and routines → new users discover Aura.

### Loop 2 — Trust Compound

Users submit reviews and outcome data (helped / didn't help) → platform intelligence improves → recommendations become more trustworthy → more users adopt Aura as their primary research tool.

### Loop 3 — Creator Integration

Beauty creators embed Aura "ingredient check" and "shade match" links in their content → new users arrive with intent → conversion to profile completion is high because users already trust the creator's recommendation of Aura.

### Loop 4 — Brand Data Flywheel (Phase 4)

Brand analytics generate revenue → fund better product catalog coverage → recommendations improve → platform is more valuable to users → more users → more data for brands.

---

## Launch Phases

### Phase 1 — Internal Alpha (Month 3)

- Internal or employee-like cohort: ~50 users
- Focus: shade matching and ingredient analysis flows
- Goal: median time-to-decision < 10 minutes, explanation helpfulness ≥ 4.0 / 5.0, and complete instrumentation for shade / reaction / return outcomes

### Phase 2 — Invite Beta (Month 5 launch, Month 5-6 ramp)

- External invite-only access scaling toward ~500 users
- Creator partnership activations
- Focus: recommendation quality, trust, and retention measurement
- Goal: show early reduction in wrong-shade, adverse-reaction, and return signals vs. self-directed baseline

### Phase 3 — Public Launch (Month 8)

- Full public availability
- Press outreach and editorial placements
- First affiliate partnerships activated
- Experimentation framework in place for outcome and trust optimization

## Monetization Timeline

| Revenue Stream           | Launch Phase   | Mechanism                                                                    |
| ------------------------ | -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Affiliate Commerce**   | Phase 2        | Commission on purchases through Aura purchase links; disclosed to users      |
| **Premium Subscription** | Phase 3+       | Enhanced AI analysis, advanced comparison, priority support                  |
| **Brand Analytics**      | Phase 4        | Anonymized aggregate insights sold to beauty brands and ingredient suppliers |

**Important:** Affiliate placements will never influence recommendation ranking. Affiliate links are generated post-rank. This is a core trust principle and must be maintained regardless of commercial pressure.

## Key Performance Indicators

### User Outcome & Trust

| KPI                                 | Target |
| ----------------------------------- | ------ |
| Median time-to-decision             | Improves across beta cohorts |
| Shade-miss rate                     | Lower than self-directed baseline in supported complexion journeys |
| Adverse reaction report rate        | Below defined safety threshold with 24-hour triage |
| Return / regret reduction           | Positive directional improvement vs. self-directed baseline |
| Explanation helpfulness rating      | ≥ 4.0 / 5.0 |

### Secondary Growth

| KPI                     | Phase 1 Target     | Phase 2 Target |
| ----------------------- | ----------------- | -------------- |
| Monthly active users    | 50 (internal alpha) | 500 (invite beta ramp) |
| Profile completion rate | ≥ 70%             | ≥ 65%          |
| D1 retention            | ≥ 50%             | ≥ 45%          |

### Revenue

| KPI                       | Phase 2 Target                          |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Affiliate conversion rate | ≥ 2% of recommendation clicks           |
| Revenue per active user   | Tracked during invite beta; formal target set after 90 days of launch data |

## Positioning Statement

**For** beauty shoppers who are overwhelmed by conflicting advice and afraid of making the wrong purchase, **Aura is** an AI-powered personal advisor **that** analyzes products against your specific skin, preferences, and values — **unlike** generic recommendation engines or paid influencer content, **Aura** explains exactly why a product is or isn't right for you.
